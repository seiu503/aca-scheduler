Medical Enrollment Appointment Scheduling

Get Covered. Stay Covered.

If you have not already done so, visit https://www.orhomecaretrust.org/2019-enrollment-materials/ to review what type of appointment you may be eligible for. If you are currently enrolled in, or eligible for, any type of medical insurance or coverage other than a Trust-approved Qualified Health Plan (QHP), you may not be eligible for Supplemental Trust benefits. Refer to your Plan Booklet, page 13 to review this information. The Supplemental Trust does not provide medical insurance.

Like last year, Open Enrollment is shorter – from November 1 to December 15, so you must act quickly to ensure you are covered!

For dates and deadlines to enroll in medical insurance through the Health Insurance Marketplace, please visit https://www.healthcare.gov/quick-guide/dates-and-deadlines/.  For dates and deadlines to enroll in Medicare, please visit https://www.medicare.gov/sign-up-change-plans/index.html.

Reporting a change in your Qualified Health Plan? You can report updates and changes year-round if you are already enrolled in a Qualified Health Plan on the Marketplace. Please review the information below.

This survey will help determine if you may qualify to schedule a Special Enrollment Period appointment, an appointment to Report a Change in Circumstance, a Medicare appointment or an Open Enrollment appointment. Take a moment to answer the questions. Once you answer the questions, you’ll be provided with a link to schedule a phone appointment with Valley Insurance Professionals or the Trust’s Healthcare Enrollment Team. Click that link and fill out the form in order to choose the appointment time that works best for you.

If you don’t see a link or if you have any questions, please call the Healthcare Enrollment Team at (503) 303-5668 (Portland Metro Area) or toll free 1-(855) 437-2694. We may need more information to help determine your next steps. If you need to leave a message, please include your name, best phone number and times to reach you, preferred language if other than English, question and whether or not we can leave a detailed message when calling you back.

Thank you!
